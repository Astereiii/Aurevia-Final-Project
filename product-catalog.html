What are chia seeds?

  • Chia seeds are tiny, nutrient-dense seeds rich in fiber, omega-3 fatty acids, protein, and antioxidants.
  • They’re commonly added to smoothies, oatmeal, yogurt, and baked goods.

How to use chia seeds?

  • You can mix them into drinks, smoothies, oatmeal, yogurt, or desserts.
  • They absorb liquid and form a gel, making them great for puddings too.

What are the health benefits of chia seeds?

  • Chia seeds help support digestion, heart health, energy, and overall wellness because they’re packed with fiber, omega-3s, and antioxidants.
