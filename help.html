Help & Support

How long is shipping? ▼
Shipping takes 3–5 business days within Metro Manila and 5–7 days for provincial areas.
How do I track my order? ▼
Go to the Orders section in your account to check the status of your purchase.
Are the products authentic? ▼
Yes, all products are verified and sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure authenticity.
Can I return a product? ▼
Yes, returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for unopened items. Contact us to initiate a return.
